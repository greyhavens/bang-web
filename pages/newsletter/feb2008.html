February 19, 2008

Bang is Back!

Three Rings is proud to announce the relaunch of Bang!Howdy!

We made several exciting changes to Bang that we hope you'll enjoy. These include:

Opening Up Indian Trading Post

Always been curious about the Indian Trading Post but never had the gold to get a ticket? The Indian Trading Post is now open to all players! Come and try out a whole other world of game scenarios and units.

Explore a new frontier!

Increasing scrip payouts

Bang was always intended to be a fun, fast-moving game. We've increased payouts for all games and bounties to bring a little more energy to the game. More information on the recent changes here

C'mon and check out the fun!

Decreasing item prices

Never thought it made sense to buy those extra cards? Never thought you'd be able to afford that fine Stetson hat? We've dropped scrip prices for almost all items in the game by 50%. Cards, which add to the fun and strategy in games, are priced even lower. Come and check out what you can now afford in the game!

Mosey over to the general store!

Introduction of the Wrangler Pass

Bang continues to be free for all players. We now have a single, simplified payment option for the additional features in the game. You can now decide to purchase the "Wrangler Pass", a one-time purchase that will give you access to additional clothes and bounties. You can read about the Wrangler Pass here.
