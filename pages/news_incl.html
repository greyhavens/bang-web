WHERE'S MY SHOTGUN?

"Git off my property!" is the sort of thing yer likely to hear comin' out of the mouth of this ornery feller. The Shotgunner don't move so fast but he packs a powerful punch.

The Shotgunner shoots a blast so wide that units to the left and right of his main target take a bit of damage as well. This comes in mighty handy when yer opponent has brought all hands and the cook down to give you a whuppin'.


The Shotgunner
Make: Human
Mode: Ground
Moves: 3    Shoots: 1
