Welcome to Frontier Town
Howdy Pardner and welcome to the Saloon here in Frontier Town. This is where ya 'kin meet up with other folks to play games!

If this is yer first time in town, be sure to take a look around:
– The General Store is where ya can pick up Cards to use when playin' games. You can also pick up some of them Fancy Duds that ya might see other pardners wearin'.
– At the Barber you kin git yerself a fancy new haircut and put together a whole new Look to show off on yer Wanted Poster.
– The Ranch is where you kin learn more about all the different Big Shots and Units that ya use when playin' games.
– The Bank is where ya go to pick yerself up some Gold Coins which ya need to buy the real fancy stuff.

Now hit that big Play! button over there and git yerself on into a game!

2 vs. 2 Team Games
2 vs. 2 team games have arrived at your Back Parlor Table. Work with your teammate to defeat your opponents, both your points are combined to determine your team score.
 
Don't be a Lily Liver
Leaving a game against other players will not make you a popular cowpoke. Hightailing it out of a ranked game early will cause your ranks to drop and will reduce the amount of scrip you earn in future games. Best just to stick around 'til the end of the game, you'll still earn scrip, and maybe even learn a thing or two.
