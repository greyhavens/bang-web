Line Opens to Indian Trading Post
Yessir folks! The Triple-O Railroad Company is proud to announce the completion of their new express line from Frontier Town to the Indian Trading Post.

To celebrate this momentous event, The Triple-O Railroad Company will be offering free travel to and from Indian Trading post for a limited time. Take advantage of this great opportunity while it lasts! Head on over to the Train Station today.
NOV 13 RELEASE NOTES
We've got some usability improvements and bug fixes for ya:
– Revamped the team selection interface; added a timeout
– Made it possible to play "vs. player" cards (e.g. Discard) on a unit and the card will be played on the player that owns that unit
– Added a custom cursor and now the cursor turns into the card you're going to play when you select a card for play
– Added sound controls to the in-game options menu (activated by the "ESC" key)
– Revamped the game creation interface in the Back Parlor; added two new configuration options
– Added a Cowboy-ifying chat filter (this can be adjusted or turned off from the options menu)
– Added instructions for the Friendly Folks system for new users
– Added support for using GLSL shaders for folks with fancy graphics cards
Check the Announcements forum on the website for more details.
