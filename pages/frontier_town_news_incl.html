Check out the Top Ranked list to see who's got the faster hands in the west this week. Earn badges for being in the top 10 at the end of the week. The rankings are wiped clean every Sunday.
 
2 vs. 2 Team Games
2 vs. 2 team games have arrived at your Back Parlor Table. Work with your teammate to defeat your opponents, both your points are combined to determine your team score.
 
Don't be a Lily Liver
Leaving a game against other players will not make you a popular cowpoke. Hightailing it out of a ranked game early will cause your ranks to drop and will reduce the amount of scrip you earn in future games. Best just to stick around 'til the end of the game, you'll still earn scrip, and maybe even learn a thing or two.
