Welcome to Frontier Town
Howdy Pardner and welcome to the Saloon here in Frontier Town. This is where ya 'kin meet up with other folks to play games! Read on for the latest news or hit that big Play! button over there and git yerself on into a game!
 
Big Changes!
We've raised scrip payouts and lowered prices to make it easier for cowpokes to buy great stuff. We've also made Indian Trading Post available to all players, get yer ticket in the Train Station. Finally, we've removed gold from the game entirely and replaced it with the Wrangler Pass which is a one-time purchase that gives you access to everything in the game along with bonus goodies! More details are available in the Announcements Forum on the website.
 
Weekly Top Ranked Lists
Check out the Top Ranked list to see who's got the faster hands in the west this week. Earn badges for being in the top 10 at the end of the week. The rankings are wiped clean every Sunday.
 
Don't be a Lily Liver
Leaving a game against other players will not make you a popular cowpoke. Hightailing it out of a ranked game early will cause your ranks to drop and will reduce the amount of scrip you earn in future games. Best just to stick around 'til the end of the game, you'll still earn scrip, and maybe even learn a thing or two.
