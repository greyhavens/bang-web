Line Opens to Indian Trading Post
Yessir folks! The Triple-O Railroad Company is proud to announce the completion of their new express line from Frontier Town to the Indian Trading Post. Now every fine lady and gentleman can easily make the trip out to the trading post and sample the exotic delights collected there for their pleasure.

To celebrate this momentous event, The Triple-O Railroad Company will be offering free travel to and from Indian Trading post for a limited time. Take advantage of this great opportunity while it lasts! Head on over to the Train Station today.
