Frequently Asked Questions
When can I play it?
When it's ready pardner. We're roundin' up them dogies as fast as we can but them cows ain't all come home yet. Check back here as we'll update this site with new exciting information and sign up above and we'll email ya when we've got really good news.

How much will it cost?
Bang! Howdy ™ will be downloadable for free over the Internet and free to play with no signup or recurring subscription fee. Players will pay for Big Shot units and customizations and will buy train tickets to new towns using our microcurrency system. Prices will be announced prior to official launch and we expect that they will be downright inexpensive.

Will it work on my computer?
We will launch with support for Windows and Linux, and barring major problems, MacOS X as well. A graphics accelerator will be required but not a fancy one. Exact system requirements will be announced nearer to launch.
