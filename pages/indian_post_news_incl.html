The Frontier Town and Indian Trading Post servers will be down from 10:00 PM to 1:00 AM PDT on both Friday Sept 14th and Saturday Sept 15th.
 
Rustle up some Bounties
The Indian Trading Post Sheriff's Office is open and waiting for some brave souls to collect these new Bounties. Currently available are 5 Tin Star Town Bounties and 5 Copper Star Most Wanted Bounties. You can find the Copper Star over at the General Store.
 
Pick your Game Mode
You can now select whether you want to play Competitive or Cooperative games when doing a ranked match. Only Competitive games will affect your Overall ranking. A Cooperative game in the hideout can only be played with your fellow gang members.
 
Hideout now Open
The Hideout is now accessible from town. You can play gang matches in all the Indian Trading Post scenarios (you best put aside your differences when playing Forest Guardians). You'll also be able to chat with fellow gang members who are in the Frontier Town Hideout.
 
Hitch up to the Tradin' Post
Welcome to the Indian Trading Post! We got a whole heap of new and exciting stuff for ya to play with.

Head over to The Ranch to learn about all the new units like the Storm Caller, the Buffalo Rider, the Dream Catcher and the Dog Soldier.

Take a gander at the new cards over at The General Store. While yer there check out the spectacular Indian-themed duds we got for ya and all the fancy face paints.
 
Be sure to run through all the new tutorials to familiarize yourself with the great new gameplay scenarios: Wendigo Attack, Totem Building and Forest Guardians. That last one, Forest Guardians, is a co-op scenario, which is something we're excited to be tryin' out and if you all like it, we'll be doin' more scenarios along those lines.
