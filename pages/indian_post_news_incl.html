Hitch Up to the Indian Trading Post
Welcome to the Indian Trading Post! We got a whole heap of new and exciting stuff for ya to play with. Head over to The Ranch to learn about all the new units like the Storm Caller, the Buffalo Rider, the Dream Catcher and the Dog Soldier. Take a gander at the new cards over at The General Store. While yer there check out the spectacular Indian-themed duds we got for ya and all the fancy face paints.

Be sure to run through all the new tutorials to familiarize yourself with the great new gameplay scenarios: Wendigo Attack, Totem Building and Forest Guardians. That last one, Forest Guardians, is a co-op scenario, which is something we're excited to be tryin' out and if you all like it, we'll be doin' more scenarios along those lines.

NOV 1 RELEASE NOTES

Bein' that this is the very first release of Indian Trading Post we don't have any proper release notes other than to say: here it is! However, we'll be reportin' ITP changes here in future releases and Frontier Town changes in the Saloon over in Frontier Town.

In the meanwhile, we'd appreciate it muchly if you could give everything here a good thorough testing and be sure to report any problems you encounter by pressin' F2 and filin' a bug report. Thanks again fer all yer help.
