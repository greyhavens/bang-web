Hideout now Open
The Hideout is now accessible from town. You can play gang matches in all the Indian Trading Post scenarios (you best put aside your differences when playing Forest Guardians). You'll also be able to chat with fellow gang members who are in the Frontier Town Hideout.

Scrip Bonuses in Forest Guardians
A new scrip bonus has been added to Forest Guardians! For each successfully completed wave (where you saved at least half the trees), you will receive a scrip bonus equal to the amount of saws for that wave. For example, you finished three waves that were 4, 5 and 6 saws respectively, then everyone will get 15 extra scrip on top of their normal earnings.
