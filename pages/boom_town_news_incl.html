Welcome to Boom Town
Wow! It's pretty quiet around here

If this is yer first time in town, be sure to take a look around:
– The General Store is where ya can pick up Cards to use when playin' games. You can also pick up some of them Fancy Duds that ya might see other pardners wearin'.
– At the Barber you kin git yerself a fancy new haircut and put together a whole new Look to show off on yer Wanted Poster.
– The Ranch is where you kin learn more about all the different Big Shots and Units that ya use when playin' games.
– The Bank is where ya go to pick yerself up some Gold Coins which ya need to buy the real fancy stuff.

Now hit that big Play! button over there and git yerself on into a game!
