Grey Havens Privacy Policy

This policy covers how Grey Havens Design, LLC ("Grey Havens") treats personal information that Grey Havens collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Grey Havens products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.


Information Collection and Use

Grey Havens collects personal information when you register with Grey Havens, when you use Grey Havens products or services, and when you visit Grey Havens pages or the pages of certain Grey Havens partners. Grey Havens may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, zip code etc. Once you register with Grey Havens and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

Grey Havens collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.

Grey Havens automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Grey Havens cookie information, and the page you request.

Grey Havens uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.


Children

Puzzle Pirates is operated in two modes, Puzzle Pirates Original and Puzzle Pirates Family.

Grey Havens complies with COPPA regulations for Puzzle Pirates Family. This means that we only accept information supplied by parents which is treated as described above. We do not store information about children under 13 (for example, email addresses).

Grey Havens does not accept registrations from children under age 13 on Puzzle Pirates Original, or any of our other games.


Information Sharing and Disclosure

Grey Havens does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Grey Havens under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Grey Havens communicate with you about offers from Grey Havens and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Grey Havens's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if Grey Havens is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Grey Havens will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Cookies

Grey Havens may set and access Grey Havens cookies on your computer.


Communications from Grey Havens

If you provide us with your e-mail address, we may send you e-mail from time to time. All e-mails from Grey Havens will be strictly 'opt-out' communications, and will include a URL or email address where you can unsubscribe from future mailings.


Confidentiality and Security

Your Grey Havens Account Information is password-protected.

In certain areas Grey Havens uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions.

We take security seriously and make our best efforts to prevent compromise of your personal information. In the event of a serious security breach, we will alert our registered users.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

Grey Havens may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address specified in your Grey Havens account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.


Questions and Suggestions

If you have questions or suggestions, please email us at privacy@banghowdy.com.
