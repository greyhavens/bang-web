Troubleshooting

If this is your first time and you are having trouble starting, you probably have a firewall preventing Bang! Howdy from connecting.

  • Software Firewalls (such as Windows Firewall and Zone Alarm)
    • Ensure that "javaw.exe" is in the list of of allowed programs
  • Hardware Firewalls (such as a router)
    • Ensure that traffic is allowed on port 47624

Check the forums for more help.
