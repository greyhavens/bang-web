Game Summary

Highlights

  • Anime-influenced Steampunk Wild West setting
  • Casual hybrid between real-time and turn-based strategy
  • Online distribution: free download and free to play with in-game items purchased with microcurrency
  • Episodic content delivery: periodic expansions following launch adding new environments, items, units and gameplay modes
  • Leverages Three Rings' expertise and technology in microcurreny driven casual online games

Gameplay

  • Numerous variations on the basic theme of tactical strategy
  • Each gameplay mode defines a set of rules and goals like: stealing gold nuggets, branding cattle, building totem poles or defending a forest against megalomanaical logging robots
  • Players have teams of units, each with unique capabilities, that they use to compete (or cooperate) with other players
  • Players start the game with up to three Cards which can be activated for special powers
  • Bonuses appear during the game that players can activate for special powers or to earn additional Cards
  • Players earn Badges by achiving meta-gameplay goals (winning multiple games in a row, collecting a certain number of gold nuggets during a game, or a variety of other statistical targets)
  • Badges unlock additional features like new units, special avatar outfits and other secret stuff
  • In addition to multiplayer play, players can play a single player campaign of Bounties where they tackle increasingly challenging games versus AI opponents which can have special additional win conditions beyond those normally required by the gameplay scenarios

Episodic Delivery

  • The game is divided into five towns, each of which introduce new gameplay scenarios, unit types, avatar customizations and gameplay arenas
  • Players start in Frontier Town adn purchase Train Tickets using microcurrency to access additional towns
  • The game launched with Frontier Town and Indian Trading Post, Boom Town is currently under development and two additional towns are planned to follow

Revenue Model

  • Bang! Howdy is downloadable and free to play, players purchase in-game items using Three Rings' microcurrency engine (which is currently used for Puzzle Pirates)
  • Players earn Scrip (in-game cash) by playing games; Scrip can be used in combination with Gold (microcurrency purchased for real money) to buy in-game items like new units, avatar customizations and access to additional Bounties
  • Gold and Scrip can be traded by players on an in-game currency exchange, allowing players to balance their investments of time and money to best fit their means and preference

Numbers!

  • Five different themed towns: Frontier Town, Indian Trading Post, Boom Town, Ghost Town and the City of Gold
  • A single player campaign comprised of hundreds of Bounty games
  • Fifteen engaging and innovative Gameplay Modes
  • Over 30 unique Units, each with special powers
  • Dozens of Cards and Bonuses that spice up the strategy gameplay
  • Hundreds of themed Gameplay Arenas
  • Hundreds of Badges, many of which unlock features and other surprises
  • Hundreds of Avatar outfits and accessories
