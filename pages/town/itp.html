I'm Wrasslin' Bull from the Indian Trading Post. I'm mighty pleased to present to ya some new units and scenarios here at Bang! Howdy's second town. Stop by my Ranch to meet our new Big Shots - they're truly folk to ride the river with. Also, don't forget to mosey on down to Hagglin' Greyhawk's General Store and stock up on cards and gear that are only available at Indian Trading Post!

The Buffalo Rider attacks by ramming into her enemies. the further away she starts from her target, the more powerful her attack. Woe betide anyone caught between a charging Buffalo Rider and a hard place.

She has good range and power, but cannot attack flying units.

Special Power:If she starts at least four tiles away when attacking, she will push her target backwards out of their current spot.

The Storm Caller has a formidable ally in Mother Nature and calls down a bolt of lightning to attack his enemies. When he's around, lightning can and does strike twice.

Watch where you're aimin' though, the chaining lighting doesn't discriminate between friend and foe.

Special Power: His lightning attack chains at 80% to units standing next to his main target and at 50% to units standing next to those.

Beware the Wendigo Attack! When darkness falls, your troops must stand in the Petroglyph of Protection to survive. Only those who hold the Sacred Talisman can roam free and unscathed when the Deadly Wendigo appears.

As one of the Forest Guardians, you must stand next the saplings into order to nuture their growth. In order to save the Forest, co-operate with other teams to fend off the Logging Robots.
